My Favourite Video Games

These are my favourite video games (in no particular order):

  • Marvel's Spiderman

    The spiderman game, although a bit short, may be one of the most
    well rounded games I have ever played as it has fun gameplay,
    an emotional story and potential for expansion.

  • Horizon Zero Dawn

    Although the story is a little lackluster, this game has one of the
    most creative combat systems ever with almost limitless
    potential for tactics.

  • Bloodborne

    The game is a very challenging game with alot of potential character
    builds but also so much fun yet so painful as when you die, you
    restart from specific points and must repeat all you have done
    to regain your lost points.

  • Assassin's Creed Brotherhood

    To me, this game is the pinnacle of an iconic game series and the game
    that started me on the path of gaming in the first place which is
    why I love it.

  • God Of War

    A continuation to an iconic series, this game also masters every element
    in a game from a thrilling story, great character development and
    the gameplay is just phenomenal. I'm also a fan of norse mythology.
